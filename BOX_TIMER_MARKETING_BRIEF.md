# Box Timer - Marketing Brief

This document contains everything needed to create a marketing website for Box Timer, an iOS workout timer app.

---

## App Overview

**Box Timer** is a purpose-built workout timer app for iPhone and iPad. It's designed for athletes, fitness enthusiasts, boxers, CrossFit athletes, and anyone doing interval training who wants a simple, distraction-free timer.

**Core Philosophy**: Simple, clean, ad-free. No unnecessary complexity.

**Made in Sweden**

---

## Three Timer Modes

### 1. Count Up Timer
- Starts from 00:00 and counts upward
- Set an optional time cap (up to 99 minutes)
- Perfect for tracking open-ended activities like stretching, mobility work, or endurance sessions

### 2. Count Down Timer
- Set your target duration and watch it count down
- Configurable from seconds up to 99 minutes
- Audio alerts when complete
- Ideal for timed exercises, planks, holds, or rest periods

### 3. Interval Timer (The Star Feature)
- Work/Rest cycle training
- Configure work duration, rest duration, and number of rounds (1-99)
- Visual round indicator shows current round
- Audio cues: beeps during final seconds of rest, long beep when new round starts
- Perfect for HIIT, Tabata, boxing rounds, CrossFit WODs, and circuit training

## Key Features

### Zero Ads
- Completely ad-free experience
- No banner ads, no video ads, no interruptions
- Focus entirely on your workout

### Optional 10-Second Countdown
- Get ready before your timer starts
- Audio countdown beeps on 3, 2, 1
- Can be toggled on/off per preference

### Customizable Audio
- Toggle sound effects on/off
- Adjustable volume (0-100%)
- Clear 2000Hz beeps that cut through gym noise
- Works alongside your music (doesn't pause other audio)

### Three Display Themes
1. **Standard**: Green and red with glow effect - classic digital look
2. **Minimal**: Green and red without glow - clean and simple
3. **High Contrast**: Blue and orange - optimized for visibility and accessibility

### Workout History
- Every completed timer automatically saved
- Pin favorite workouts for quick access
- Rename workouts with custom names
- Filter by timer type
- Tap any history item to instantly reload those settings
- Smart date display (Today, weekday names, full dates)

### Multi-Language Support
- English
- German (Deutsch)
- Spanish (Español)
- French (Français)
- Polish (Polski)
- Swedish (Svenska)
